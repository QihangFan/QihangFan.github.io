Narrative

Chapter III: Rethink the Factory Typology

In order to get out of the factory stereotype, we turn to something familiar for spatial inspiration. We start to imagine what a designer's daily routine would be, and extract entertaining spaces that they would enjoy to stay in.

With a human-centered approach, the summarized upcycling processes are grouped and linked to new architecture typologies. Each area distinguishable and specific to its function.
